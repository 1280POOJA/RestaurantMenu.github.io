Silver Dining :

It is not just about the food but it encompasses a lot more than what one can visualise.Built on the sole belief that exquiste food should be enjoyed in serene & cozy environment, the menu has been carefully crafted to provide you with a rich and varied experience.

An indulgent choice of creative cuisine, ranging from original Chinese, Japanese, Indian, and Continental menus, crafted authentically, suiting everyone's taste. The cuisine is the handiwork of a gifted culinary team and accentuates on world-class epicurian experiences besides bespoke service, as they fuse to create a menu reflecting refined tastes and character. creations using only the freshest ingredients, Silver Dining strives to push cretive boundaries in the spirit of celebrating global cuisines.

How To Reach Us :

  • From Goa Airport(Dabolin)

    1. Go to Vasco KTC Bus Stop just outside the airport
    2. Take Bus No. 765 for the anjuna Stop
    3. After 25 min ride you will reach at Anjuna Beach
    4. Silver Dining is 150m from bus stop towards the beach
